Research

After a couple of years working in the area of Business Process Management (BPM), nowadays, my research efforts are focusing on understanding the role of ICT in promoting the active participation of citizens in the democratic life. In particular, I'm studying how social networking services (e.g. Facebook, Twitter, Google+) can be employed to empower large-scale ideation, deliberation, and decision-making processes for civic engagement.
